# The Baking Buddy

Baking Buddy is an AI-powered mobile companion that guides home bakers through every stage of their baking journey, from pantry to plate. Tell Baking Buddy what ingredients you have on hand and it will suggest recipes you can make right now.  Follow a recipe, snap a photo of your finished bake, and the app's AI analyzer will evaluate your crust, crumb, and texture, explaining what went right and how to improve next time. Planning ahead? Baking Buddy converts any recipe into a clean, categorized grocery list in seconds. 
And if you ever find yourself mid-bake and missing a key ingredient, the built-in substitution guide instantly tells you what to use and in what ratio, so your bake never has to stop. Baking Buddy isn't four separate tools bolted together; it's one intelligent baking companion designed to meet you wherever you are in the process.
